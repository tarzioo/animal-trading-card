Brown Bear

The tips of the hair on a brown bear look grayish or grizzly.

  • Scientific Name: Ursus arctos
  • Average Weight: 180-1,300 lbs
  • Average Lifespan: 35 years
  • Average Height: 7 feet tall

During the fall, brown bears eat practically around the clock, stocking up for the next four to seven months. They may eat 90 pounds of food per day.
